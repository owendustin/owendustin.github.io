  <div class="content">
    <p>This is just a simple lid that I made for my engineering design team. Its purpose is to seal around a battery casing and it will later be lined with carbon fibre.</p>

    <p>I started by taking a bunch of measurements to get good dimensions for everything and then I made a quick cad model from which I could reference my cuts from.</p>

    <img src="img/lid/image1.png" alt="image1">

    <p>I then bought all of the metal and used a corded grinder to create miters on all four corners. I used a magnet alignment tool to ensure everything was square before using my MIG welder to tack and weld everything into place.</p>
